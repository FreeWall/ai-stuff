### Plan a task

1. Ask me what task you should plan.
2. Ask me as many questions as you need to fill out any gaps.
3. Plan the task in a `plan.md` artifact.

Example of plan:
```markdown
# Plan of "Title"

## Summary

Summary of the task

<br>

## Steps

1. Step 1
2. Step 2
3. Step 3
```
